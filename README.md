## 🚨 The Problem

> 🏦 Most **bank statements**, 🏥 **medical bills**, and 📃 **essential documents** arrive as PDFs or Word files.  
> Over **60%** of these are **inaccessible** to screen readers:
>
> - ❌ Missing semantic tags  
> - ❌ Improper reading order  
> - ❌ Unlabeled or broken form elements

⚠️ For individuals who rely on screen readers, this creates a **critical barrier** in handling daily life—accessing legal documents, healthcare forms, and financial records becomes impossible without help.

---

## 💡 The Solution

> **“Fix Accessibility at the Source — Instantly, Intelligently, and Inclusively.”**

Meet the **Accessibility Analyzer & Fixer** — a **cloud-based AI tool** that takes any document and makes it accessible:

✅ Upload PDF / DOCX / PPT  
✅ Detects and tags paragraphs, headers, tables  
✅ Corrects reading order automatically  
✅ Labels form fields intelligently  
✅ Exports optional HTML or screen-reader-friendly versions  
✅ Offers summarized **voice-readable previews**
